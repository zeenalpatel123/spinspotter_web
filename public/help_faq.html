Help Center

Welcome to the SpinSpotter Help Center! We're here to assist you in making the most of our platform for sports ground bookings and management. Browse through our frequently asked questions or reach out to our support team for personalized help.

General Questions

What is SpinSpotter?

SpinSpotter is a platform that connects sports enthusiasts with ground owners, enabling easy and efficient booking for sports grounds and tournaments.

How does SpinSpotter work?

Users can search for sports grounds, compare facilities, check availability, and book them directly through our platform. Ground owners can list their properties and manage bookings seamlessly.

Is SpinSpotter free to use?

Creating an account and browsing grounds is free. Booking fees may vary based on the ground and services offered.

Account and Registration

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Sign Up" button, fill in your details, and verify your email to create an account.

What should I do if I forget my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.

Can I update my account information?

Yes, go to your profile settings to update your personal information, payment methods, and preferences.

Booking Questions

How do I book a sports ground?

Use the search bar to find a ground by location or sport, select a date and time, and proceed to payment to confirm your booking.

Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?

Yes, cancellations and rescheduling are allowed based on the ground's cancellation policy. Check the policy before confirming your booking.

Will I get a refund if I cancel my booking?

Refunds depend on the cancellation policy of the ground. Some grounds offer full refunds if canceled within a specific timeframe.

For Ground Owners

How can I list my ground on SpinSpotter?

Sign up as a ground owner, fill out the listing details, add photos, and submit it for approval. Once approved, your ground will be live for bookings.

Can I set custom pricing for my ground?

Yes, you can customize pricing based on peak hours, weekends, and additional services.

How do I manage bookings?

Log in to your dashboard to view, confirm, or modify bookings. You can also communicate with users directly through the platform.

Payments and Billing

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and UPI payments.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, all transactions are secured with industry-standard encryption and data protection protocols.

How do I get a receipt for my booking?

Receipts are automatically sent to your registered email upon payment confirmation.

Account and Registration

What should I do if I encounter an error during booking?

Try refreshing the page and attempting the booking again. If the issue persists, contact our support team with details of the error.

My payment failed. What should I do?

Check your payment details and try again. If the issue continues, contact your bank or our support team for assistance.
