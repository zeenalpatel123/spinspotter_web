What is Game Circle?

Game Circle is a unique feature on SpinSpotter that brings players and ground owners together for seamless sports activity planning. Whether you're organizing a casual game with friends or hosting a tournament, Game Circle helps you connect, book, and play.

Features of Game Circle

Simplified Booking

Effortlessly search, select, and book your preferred ground in minutes without any complications.

Team Invitations

Easily invite teammates, track RSVPs, and keep everyone informed about match details.

Customizable Match Settings

Adjust match timings, assign player roles, and tailor settings to suit your team’s needs.

Real-Time Notifications

Keep your team updated with instant reminders, alerts, and last-minute changes.

Multi-Sport Support

Whether it’s cricket, football, tennis, or more, Game Circle supports every sport you love.
